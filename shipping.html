At Sorsone, we are committed to delivering your orders quickly, safely, and efficiently. This Shipping and Delivery Policy outlines our shipping methods, delivery timelines, charges, and other important information about getting your premium oversized streetwear to your doorstep.

Quick Summary: We ship across India with standard delivery in 5-7 business days. Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Free shipping on orders above ₹1,999.

1. SHIPPING COVERAGE

1.1 Serviceable Areas

We currently ship to all serviceable pin codes across India. Our shipping partners cover:

  • All major cities and metro areas
  • Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities
  • Most rural and remote areas (delivery times may vary)
  • Union Territories

1.2 Non-Serviceable Areas

Unfortunately, we cannot currently ship to the following locations:

  • PO Box addresses
  • Military/Army addresses (APO/FPO)
  • Areas deemed high-risk or inaccessible by our logistics partners
  • International destinations (we only ship within India)

You can check if your pin code is serviceable by entering it at checkout. If your area is not currently serviceable, please contact us at [email protected], and we'll work to find an alternative solution.

2. ORDER PROCESSING TIME

2.1 Standard Processing

All orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days (Monday through Saturday, excluding public holidays) after payment confirmation. Processing includes:

  • Payment verification and authorization
  • Inventory allocation and product picking
  • Quality check and packaging
  • Generating shipping labels and documentation

2.2 Orders Placed on Weekends and Holidays

Orders placed on Sundays or public holidays will be processed on the next business day. For example:

  • Orders placed on Sunday will begin processing on Monday
  • Orders placed on Saturday may begin processing the same day or Monday, depending on warehouse operations

2.3 High-Demand Periods

During sale events, festive seasons, or promotional periods, order processing may take 2-3 business days due to high order volumes. We will notify you via email if there are any delays in processing your order.

3. SHIPPING METHODS AND DELIVERY TIME

3.1 Standard Shipping

Our standard shipping method offers the best balance of speed and cost:

  • Delivery Time: 5-7 business days after order dispatch
  • Coverage: Pan-India delivery to all serviceable pin codes
  • Tracking: Full tracking provided via SMS and email
  • Signature: Delivery may require signature confirmation

3.2 Delivery Timeline Factors

Actual delivery time may vary based on:

  • Location: Metro cities receive deliveries faster (3-5 days) than remote areas (7-10 days)
  • Weather Conditions: Adverse weather may cause delays
  • Public Holidays: Deliveries are not made on Sundays and public holidays
  • Natural Disasters: Force majeure events may impact delivery schedules
  • Courier Availability: Peak season congestion at courier facilities

We occasionally offer special shipping promotions, including:

5. ORDER TRACKING

5.1 Tracking Information

Once your order is shipped, you will receive:

  • Shipping confirmation email with tracking number
  • SMS notification with tracking link
  • Estimated delivery date
  • Courier partner name and contact information

5.2 How to Track Your Order

You can track your order through multiple methods:

  • Website: Log into your account and navigate to "Order History" to view tracking information
  • Email Link: Click the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email
  • SMS Link: Click the link in your tracking SMS
  • Courier Website: Visit the courier partner's website and enter your tracking number
  • Contact Us: Call +91 7907090396 or email [email protected] with your order number

5.3 Tracking Updates

You will receive tracking updates at key milestones:

  • Order shipped from warehouse
  • In transit to your city
  • Out for delivery
  • Delivered successfully
  • Delivery attempted (if you were unavailable)

6. DELIVERY PROCESS

6.1 Delivery Attempt

Our courier partners will make up to 3 delivery attempts:

  • First Attempt: Delivery to the address provided during checkout
  • Second Attempt: If you're unavailable, a second attempt will be made the next business day
  • Third Attempt: A final attempt will be made, and you'll be contacted via phone

If all three delivery attempts fail, the package will be returned to our warehouse, and you will be responsible for re-shipping charges if you wish to receive the order again.

6.2 Delivery Requirements

To ensure successful delivery:

  • Provide a complete and accurate shipping address with landmark
  • Include a valid contact phone number
  • Ensure someone is available to receive the package during business hours (10 AM - 7 PM)
  • Provide identification if requested by the delivery personnel
  • Sign the delivery receipt upon receiving your order

6.3 Unattended Delivery

For security reasons, we do not support unattended deliveries. If you will not be available to receive your order:

  • Arrange for a trusted person to receive on your behalf
  • Provide alternate delivery instructions in the order notes
  • Contact the courier partner to reschedule delivery

7. PACKAGE INSPECTION AT DELIVERY

We strongly recommend that you inspect your package at the time of delivery:

  • Check the package for any visible damage or tampering
  • If the package appears damaged, note it on the delivery receipt before signing
  • You have the right to refuse delivery if the package is severely damaged
  • Open the package in the presence of delivery personnel if you notice any damage
  • Take photographs of damaged packaging for reference

If you discover any issues after accepting delivery, please contact us immediately at [email protected] with photographs within 48 hours.

8. FAILED DELIVERY SCENARIOS

8.1 Incorrect Address

If your order cannot be delivered due to an incomplete or incorrect address:

  • Contact us immediately at +91 7907090396 or [email protected]
  • We will attempt to update the address with our courier partner
  • Additional shipping charges may apply for address corrections
  • If correction is not possible, the order will be returned, and you'll need to place a new order

8.2 Refused Delivery

If you refuse delivery without valid reason:

  • The order will be returned to our warehouse
  • Refund will be processed minus shipping charges and a 15% restocking fee
  • Refund processing will take 10-14 business days after we receive the returned package

8.3 Customer Unavailability

If you are unavailable for all three delivery attempts:

  • The order will be marked as RTO (Return to Origin)
  • A full refund will be processed minus two-way shipping charges
  • You may place a new order and pay fresh shipping charges

9. ORDER MODIFICATION AND ADDRESS CHANGES

9.1 Before Shipment

If you need to modify your order or change the delivery address before shipment:

  • Contact us immediately at [email protected] or +91 7907090396
  • Provide your order number and the changes required
  • We will attempt to make changes if the order hasn't been processed
  • Once shipped, address changes are at the courier's discretion and may incur additional charges

9.2 After Shipment

Once your order is shipped, we cannot guarantee modification requests. However, we will:

  • Contact our courier partner on your behalf
  • Attempt to redirect the shipment (additional charges may apply)
  • If redirection is not possible, you may need to refuse delivery and place a new order

10. MULTIPLE ORDERS

If you place multiple orders in quick succession:

  • Each order will be processed and shipped separately
  • You will receive separate tracking numbers for each order
  • Orders cannot be combined after placement
  • Contact us if you wish to modify multiple orders before processing

11. BULK ORDERS

For bulk orders (more than 10 units of the same product):

  • Please contact us at [email protected] before placing the order
  • We can arrange special shipping rates and faster processing
  • Bulk orders may require 3-5 business days for processing
  • Corporate gifting and custom packaging options available

12. LOST OR DAMAGED SHIPMENTS

12.1 Lost Packages

If your tracking shows the package as delivered but you haven't received it:

  • Check with neighbors, family members, or building security
  • Verify the delivery address on your order confirmation
  • Contact the courier partner using the tracking number
  • If still not found, contact us within 7 days at [email protected]
  • We will investigate with the courier and arrange a replacement or refund

12.2 Damaged Packages

If you receive a damaged package:

  • Note the damage on the delivery receipt before signing
  • Take photographs of the damaged packaging and product
  • Contact us immediately at [email protected] with photos
  • We will arrange for a replacement or full refund
  • Return shipping will be covered by Sorsone

13. PEAK SEASON AND DELAYS

During peak shopping seasons (festive periods, sale events), delivery may experience delays due to:

  • High order volumes across all e-commerce platforms
  • Increased traffic congestion in major cities
  • Courier facility capacity constraints
  • Weather-related disruptions

We will proactively communicate any expected delays via email and update estimated delivery dates. We appreciate your patience during these busy periods.

14. COVID-19 AND SAFETY MEASURES

In light of ongoing health concerns, we maintain the following safety protocols:

  • All products are sanitized before packaging
  • Warehouse staff follow strict hygiene and safety guidelines
  • Contactless delivery options available (delivery at doorstep without signature)
  • Delivery personnel follow safety protocols mandated by health authorities

15. INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING

We currently do not ship internationally. We only deliver within India to all serviceable pin codes. We are working to expand our shipping capabilities and will announce international shipping when available.

  • Keep your contact number active and reachable
  • Be available during delivery hours or arrange for someone to receive on your behalf
  • Inspect packages upon delivery and report issues immediately
  • Contact us promptly if you need to modify your order
