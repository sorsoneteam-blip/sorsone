Shipping Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

1. Shipping Coverage

We currently ship Silicon Ice Rollers across India. Delivery is available to all serviceable pin codes as per our logistics partner's network.

2. Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email. You will receive a tracking number via SMS and email once your order has been shipped.

3. Shipping Methods and Delivery Time

Standard Shipping (Free)

  • Delivery Time: 3-7 business days
  • Coverage: All India
  • Cost: FREE for all orders
  • Tracking: Provided via SMS and Email

Express Shipping

  • Delivery Time: 1-3 business days
  • Coverage: Major cities only
  • Cost: ‚Çπ99 (if available for your location)
  • Tracking: Real-time tracking available

4. Shipping Charges

We offer FREE SHIPPING on all orders across India. There are no hidden charges or minimum order requirements.

5. Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive:

  • Shipping confirmation email with tracking number
  • SMS notification with tracking link
  • Real-time tracking updates

You can track your order by clicking the tracking link sent to your email/SMS or by contacting our customer support.

6. Delivery Locations

We Ship To:

  • All major cities and towns in India
  • Rural areas (as per courier serviceability)
  • PO Box addresses (where supported)

We Do NOT Ship To:

  • International addresses (currently India only)
  • Non-serviceable pin codes

7. Shipping Address

Please ensure that you provide the complete and correct shipping address at the time of placing your order. We are not responsible for orders shipped to incorrect addresses provided by the customer.

Address should include:

  • Full name of recipient
  • Complete street address with house/building number
  • City, State, and PIN code
  • Contact phone number
  • Landmark (if applicable)

8. Failed Delivery Attempts

If delivery fails due to incorrect address, unavailability of recipient, or refusal to accept the package:

  • Our courier partner will make 2-3 delivery attempts
  • You will be notified via SMS/email for each attempt
  • If all attempts fail, the order will be returned to us
  • Re-shipping charges may apply for re-delivery

9. Customs, Duties, and Taxes

All prices displayed on our website are inclusive of applicable taxes. There are no additional customs or duties for domestic orders within India.

10. Damaged or Lost Packages

Damaged in Transit:

  • Please inspect your package upon delivery
  • If damaged, refuse delivery or note the damage on delivery receipt
  • Contact us within 24 hours with photos of the damaged product and packaging
  • We will arrange for a replacement or refund

Lost in Transit:

  • If your order doesn't arrive within the estimated delivery time
  • Contact us immediately
  • We will investigate with our logistics partner
  • A replacement or full refund will be provided if the package is confirmed lost

11. Order Changes and Cancellations

Before Shipping:

  • You can modify or cancel your order within 6 hours of placing it
  • Contact our support team immediately
  • Full refund will be processed if cancelled before shipping

After Shipping:

  • Orders cannot be modified once shipped
  • You can refuse delivery and request a refund
  • Return shipping charges may apply

12. Holidays and Peak Seasons

During major holidays and festive seasons (Diwali, Christmas, New Year, etc.), delivery times may be extended by 2-4 days due to high order volumes. We will notify you of any delays.
