FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How long does shipping take?

We process orders within 1-2 business days. Delivery typically takes 5-7 business days for standard shipping across India. For more details, visit our Shipping Policy page.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy for unused items in original condition with tags attached. Please review our Return & Refund Policy for complete details.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also contact us at [email protected] with your order number.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and digital wallets through our secure payment gateway powered by Razorpay.

How do I choose the right size?

Our oversized collection is designed for a relaxed, comfortable fit. We recommend checking the specific measurements provided on each product page. If you're between sizes, we suggest sizing down for a more fitted oversized look.
