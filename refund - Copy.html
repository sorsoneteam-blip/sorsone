Refund & Return Policy

Last Updated: January 16, 2026

At Sorsone, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your Silicon Ice Roller, we're here to help.

1. Return Eligibility

You may return your product within 7 days of receiving your order for a full refund or exchange, provided that:

  • The product is unused and in its original condition
  • The product is in its original packaging
  • All tags and labels are intact
  • You have the original receipt or proof of purchase

2. Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene and safety reasons, we cannot accept returns on:

  • Opened or used products
  • Products with broken seals or damaged packaging
  • Sale or clearance items (unless defective)

3. How to Initiate a Return

To start a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact us at info@sorsone.com or call +1 (234) 567-890
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. Wait for return authorization and shipping instructions
  4. Pack the item securely in its original packaging
  5. Ship the item to the address provided

4. Return Shipping

Customer Responsibility: You are responsible for return shipping costs unless the product is defective or we made an error.

Shipping Insurance: We recommend using a trackable shipping service or purchasing shipping insurance. We cannot guarantee receipt of your returned item.

5. Refund Process

Processing Time

Once we receive your returned item, we will:

  • Inspect the product within 2-3 business days
  • Send you an email confirmation of receipt
  • Process your refund if the return is approved

Refund Method

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for purchase.

Refund Timeline

  • Once approved, refunds typically take 5-10 business days to appear in your account
  • The exact timing depends on your payment provider
  • If you haven't received your refund after 10 business days, please contact us

6. Exchanges

If you would like to exchange your product for a different color variant:

  • Contact us within 7 days of receiving your order
  • Return the original product following the return process
  • We'll ship the new color variant once we receive the return
  • No additional shipping charges for exchanges (if product is unused)

7. Damaged or Defective Products

If you receive a damaged or defective product:

  • Contact us immediately with photos of the damage
  • We'll arrange for a replacement or full refund
  • We'll cover all return shipping costs
  • No questions asked - customer satisfaction is our priority

8. Wrong Item Received

If you receive the wrong product:

  • Contact us within 7 days with your order number
  • We'll arrange for pickup of the wrong item
  • We'll ship the correct item at no additional cost
  • You may keep the wrong item if it's not cost-effective to return

9. Cancellations

Before Shipping

You may cancel your order before it ships for a full refund. Contact us as soon as possible.

After Shipping

Once an order has shipped, it cannot be cancelled. You may return the item following our standard return policy once you receive it.

10. Partial Refunds

In some cases, only partial refunds may be granted:

  • Products with obvious signs of use
  • Products returned more than 7 days after delivery
  • Products not in original packaging
  • Products with missing parts not due to our error

11. Restocking Fee

We do not charge any restocking fees for standard returns (unused products in original packaging).

12. International Returns

Currently, we only ship within India. For any international orders (if applicable), customers are responsible for all return shipping costs and any customs fees.

13. Lost or Stolen Packages

We are not responsible for packages lost or stolen after delivery confirmation. We recommend:

  • Tracking your shipment
  • Requiring signature confirmation
  • Using a secure delivery location

14. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Refund Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: info@sorsone.com
  • Phone: +1 (234) 567-890
  • Business Hours: Monday-Saturday, 9AM-6PM IST
  • Website: www.sorsone.com

We're committed to your satisfaction! If you have any concerns about your purchase, please don't hesitate to reach out. We're here to help and want to ensure you have the best experience possible with Sorsone products.
